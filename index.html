✦ About Us

Cosmic Creations

Cosmic Creations is a dynamic business consulting and strategic development firm promoted by Siva Jat Reddy, an educator, business theme visualizer, business architect, and business consultant with extensive experience in transforming ideas into successful institutions and enterprises.

With a deep understanding of industry dynamics, organizational development, and creative business strategies, Cosmic Creations serves as a catalyst for growth, innovation, and sustainable success. The organization specializes in conceptualizing, designing, developing, and scaling businesses across multiple sectors, helping entrepreneurs, institutions, and organizations achieve their long-term goals.

At Cosmic Creations, every business is viewed as a unique ecosystem that requires thoughtful planning, strategic architecture, innovative ideas, and effective execution. The firm combines creativity, business intelligence, and practical solutions to deliver measurable outcomes.

Cosmic Online Tutorial Lab

“Mother is the First Teacher”

Cosmic Online Tutorial Lab is an innovative educational initiative developed with the philosophy that a mother is the first and most influential teacher in every child’s life. This project is designed to integrate the nurturing qualities of motherhood with modern learning methodologies, creating an educational environment filled with love, care, values, and meaningful learning experiences.

Cosmic Studio

Academy for Creative Arts & Skill Development

Cosmic Studio is a multidisciplinary skill development academy dedicated to nurturing artistic talent and empowering individuals through creative education. The studio serves as a platform where passion meets professional training, enabling students to develop artistic skills, confidence, personality, and career opportunities.

The academy offers training in multiple creative disciplines, combining traditional art forms with contemporary skills required in today’s creative industries.

Training Areas

Classical Dance

Preserving and promoting India’s rich cultural heritage through systematic training in classical dance forms.

Western Dance

Providing modern dance education that develops creativity, stage confidence, and performance excellence.

Modeling & Personality

Training aspiring models in grooming, confidence building, presentation skills, and professional etiquette.

Makeup & Beauty

Offering practical knowledge and professional techniques in makeup artistry and beauty enhancement.

Allied Creative Skills

Additional programs and workshops to support artistic growth, communication, stage presentation, and creative career development.
